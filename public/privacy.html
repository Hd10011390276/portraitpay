Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

🛡️ Data Protection Compliance

PortraitPay AI complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and China's Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL). Your portrait and identity data is encrypted, segregated, and handled with strict controls.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, nationality — collected during KYC verification
  • Portrait Data: Photos and face vector embeddings used for portrait certification and infringement detection
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, Alipay/WeChat Pay IDs for withdrawals
  • Blockchain Data: Ethereum wallet addresses, transaction hashes, smart contract interactions
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, and timestamps
  • Communication Data: Messages sent to our support team, feedback forms

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To perform identity verification (KYC) and prevent fraud
  • To certify portraits on the Ethereum blockchain
  • To process licensing transactions and royalty payments
  • To detect and alert you of potential portrait infringement
  • To communicate service updates and security notices
  • To comply with legal obligations under applicable laws

3. Data Minimization & Purpose Limitation

We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the purposes stated. Face vector data is extracted from portraits solely for certification and infringement detection — it is never shared with third parties or used for any other purpose. Original portrait images can be deleted at any time (see Section 9).

4. Face Vector Data Separation

PortraitPay AI stores portrait data in two separated layers:

  • Original Portrait Images: Stored in encrypted form (AES-256) in secure cloud storage. Can be deleted by user at any time.
  • Face Vector Embeddings: Mathematical representations of facial features stored separately. Required for KYC verification and infringement detection. Retained per applicable legal requirements after account closure (see Section 9).

Security Note: Face vectors cannot be reverse-engineered into a recognizable portrait image. They are one-way mathematical representations used only for matching purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EU/EEA users, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to perform our contract with you
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Explicit consent for KYC processing and marketing communications
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Fraud prevention, security, and service improvement
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Compliance with applicable laws (AML, KYC regulations)

6. PIPL Compliance (China)

For Chinese users, PortraitPay AI complies with China's Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL):

  • Personal information is processed only with individual consent
  • Clear and conspicuous notice is provided before data collection
  • Data localization: Chinese user data is stored on servers within mainland China
  • Cross-border transfers require separate consent and security assessment
  • Users have the right to access, correct, delete, and port their data
  • Sensitive personal information (face data, identity documents) requires explicit consent

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • KYC Service Providers: Authorized identity verification companies (e.g., Face++, 旷视科技) to verify identity documents
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS/Alibaba Cloud for encrypted data storage (data encrypted at rest and in transit)
  • Blockchain: Transaction data is inherently public on Ethereum — this includes wallet addresses and transaction hashes but not personal identity
  • Payment Processors: Banks, Alipay, WeChat Pay for withdrawal processing
  • Legal Compliance: Disclosure when required by law, court order, or government authority

Important: Blockchain transactions on Ethereum are public by design. Your Ethereum wallet address, transaction amounts, and timestamps will be visible on the public blockchain. This is a characteristic of decentralized blockchain technology, not a data breach.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active; deleted within 15 business days of account closure upon request
  • Original Portrait Images: Deleted immediately upon user request; retained up to 30 days in backup archives
  • Face Vector Data: Retained per legal requirements (3 years minimum per AML regulations), then deleted
  • KYC Documents: Retained 5 years per financial compliance requirements
  • Blockchain Records: Permanently retained (immutable characteristic of blockchain)
  • Transaction Records: Retained 5 years per financial compliance requirements

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right of Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right of Erasure: Request deletion of your data (see process below)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (affects future processing only)

11. Security Measures

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest; TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Face Vector Separation: Face vectors stored separately from original images
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) for all internal systems
  • Audit Logs: All data access is logged and reviewed quarterly
  • Penetration Testing: Regular third-party security audits
  • Incident Response: 24-hour notification for data breaches per GDPR Article 33

12. Cookies & Tracking

PortraitPay AI uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication, session management, security — required for the service to function
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage statistics (we use self-hosted Plausible Analytics — no third-party tracking)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use advertising or marketing tracking cookies

13. Children's Privacy

PortraitPay AI does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If we discover that data from a minor has been collected without parental consent, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child's data has been collected should contact privacy@portraitpayai.com.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. For significant changes, we will notify you via email at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, data access requests, or deletion requests, contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: privacy@portraitpayai.com
  • Address: PortraitPay AI, Pudong New District, Shanghai, China
  • EU Representative: eu-rep@portraitpayai.com (for GDPR Article 27 representation)

隐私政策

最近更新：2026年3月30日

🛡️ 数据保护合规

PortraitPay AI 遵守欧盟《通用数据保护条例》（GDPR）及中国《个人信息保护法》（PIPL）。您的肖像与身份数据均经过加密、分隔存储，并受到严格管控。

一、我们收集的信息

我们收集以下类别的信息：

  • 身份数据：姓名、电子邮件、电话号码、出生日期、国籍 — 于 KYC 验证时收集
  • 肖像数据：用于肖像认证及侵权检测的照片与人脸向量数据
  • 财务数据：银行账户信息、支付宝/微信支付ID，用于提现
  • 区块链数据：以太坊钱包地址、交易哈希、智能合约交互记录
  • 使用数据：IP地址、浏览器类型、设备标识符、访问页面及时间戳
  • 通信数据：发送给支持团队的讯息、反馈表单

二、我们如何使用您的信息

  • 创建和管理您的账户
  • 进行身份验证（KYC）及欺诈防范
  • 在以太坊区块链上认证肖像
  • 处理授权交易与版税支付
  • 检测并提醒您潜在的肖像侵权行为
  • 传达服务更新及安全通知
  • 遵守适用法律规定的合规义务

三、数据最小化与目的限制

我们仅收集严格必要的数据用于所述目的。人脸向量数据仅用于认证与侵权检测目的，绝不与第三方共享或用于任何其他用途。原始肖像图片可随时删除（见第九条）。

四、人脸向量数据分离

PortraitPay AI 将肖像数据分两层分离存储：

  • 原始肖像图片：以加密形式（AES-256）存储于安全云存储中。用户可随时删除。
  • 人脸向量嵌入：以数学形式表示面部特征，单独存储。用于KYC验证与侵权检测。账户关闭后按适用法律要求保留（见第九条）。

安全说明：人脸向量无法被逆向工程还原为可识别的肖像图片。它们是单向数学表示，仅用于匹配目的。

五、处理依据（GDPR）

对于欧盟/欧洲经济区的用户，我们依据以下法律依据处理个人数据：

  • 合同履行（第6(1)(b)条）：为履行与您的合同所必需的处理
  • 同意（第6(1)(a)条）：KYC处理和营销通信的明确同意
  • 合法利益（第6(1)(f)条）：欺诈防范、安全保障和服务改进
  • 法律义务（第6(1)(c)条）：遵守适用法律（反洗钱、KYC法规）

六、《个人信息保护法》合规（中国）

对于中国用户，PortraitPay AI 遵守中国《个人信息保护法》（PIPL）：

  • 个人信息须经个人同意方可处理
  • 在收集数据前提供清晰显著的告知
  • 数据本地化：中国用户数据存储于中国大陆境内服务器
  • 跨境传输需另行获得同意并进行安全评估
  • 用户享有访问、更正、删除和携带其数据的权利
  • 敏感个人信息（人脸数据、身份证明文件）须获得明确同意

七、数据共享与第三方

我们不出售您的个人数据。数据仅在以下情况下共享：

  • KYC 服务提供商：授权身份验证公司（如旷视科技Face++）用于验证身份文件
  • 云基础设施：AWS/阿里云用于加密数据存储（静态加密和传输加密）
  • 区块链：交易数据在以太坊上本质上公开 — 包括钱包地址和交易哈希，但不包含个人身份信息
  • 支付处理商：银行、支付宝、微信支付用于处理提现
  • 法律合规：法律、法院命令或政府机关要求时的披露

重要提示：以太坊上的区块链交易按设计是公开的。您的以太坊钱包地址、交易金额和时间戳将在公共区块链上可见。这是去中心化区块链技术的特性，不是数据泄露。

八、数据保留

  • 账户数据：账户活跃期间保留；收到关闭请求后15个工作日内删除
  • 原始肖像图片：用户请求后立即删除；备份存档中保留最多30天
  • 人脸向量数据：按法律要求保留（反洗钱法规要求最少3年），随后删除
  • KYC 文档：按金融合规要求保留5年
  • 区块链记录：永久保留（区块链不可变特性）
  • 交易记录：按金融合规要求保留5年

九、您的权利

您对个人数据享有以下权利：

  • 访问权：请求获取我们持有的关于您的所有个人数据的副本
  • 更正权：请求更正不准确的数据
  • 删除权：请求删除您的数据（流程见下文）
  • 拒绝权：拒绝基于合法利益的数据处理
  • 携带权：以结构化、机器可读的格式接收您的数据
  • 撤回同意权：随时撤回同意（仅影响未来的处理）
